💰 Crop Prices

Approximate market prices (per quintal):

  • 🌾 Rice: ₹2,100
  • 🌽 Maize: ₹1,850
  • 🥔 Potato: ₹1,400
  • 🌿 Mustard: ₹5,400

🏛 Government Schemes

  • PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi: ₹6,000 annual income support for small farmers.
  • Soil Health Card: Information on soil nutrients for better crop yield.
  • PM Fasal Bima Yojana: Crop insurance to protect farmers from loss.
  • eNAM: Online National Agriculture Market for selling crops.

📘 About This Project

Smart Kisan Connect is a student-led initiative of PMSHRI KV IIT Kharagpur to help farmers access weather forecasts, market prices, and government schemes easily in one platform. The goal is to build a Samriddh Bharat — a prosperous, self-reliant India powered by informed farmers.
